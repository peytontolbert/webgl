This is a standalone prototype entrypoint for the “Earth street-level” mode. Current feature: load small GeoJSON overlays from assets/datasets/ (including OSM bbox exports).
OSM Virginia extract downloaded to: data/osm/virginia-latest.osm.pbf (not parsed/rendered yet).
Tip: pick OSM VA (Richmond small) — streets + buildings (scene) to validate “street level”.
Left-drag: rotate Mouse wheel: zoom WASD: move on ground plane Q/E: down/up
